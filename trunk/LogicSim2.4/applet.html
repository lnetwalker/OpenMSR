LogicSim Applet


Some functions like loading and saving are disabled when running as applet.

How to use LogicSim applet

You need to upload the complete LogicSim installation directory to your webserver, especially the directories languages, circuits, modules and doc. Open LogicSim/applet.html in webbrowser.

Applet parameters:

  • loadcircuit: filename of circuit to load on start-up
  • startsimulation=true: run simulation on start-up
To change other settings like language or gate design, you can upload logicsim.cfg to the webserver.
